Overview

Managing a modern facility requires more than just repairs; it requires oversight. We provide comprehensive operational support, embedding expert teams to manage the technical and day-to-day requirements of your building.

What is Facility Management & Operational Support?

Facility Management & Operational Support refers to the maintenance, repair, and optimization of comprehensive building operations and management within hospitality and commercial environments across Saudi Arabia and the GCC. Capital Solutions provides expert support to ensure these systems operate reliably, efficiently, and in compliance with local regulations.

Scope of Services

Embedded technical team deployment
Maintenance schedule planning
Asset register management
Subcontractor supervision
Energy management audits
Helpdesk and reporting support

Common Challenges We Address

High operational overheads

Inconsistent service levels

Our Approach

We act as your technical partner. By taking ownership of the maintenance lifecycle, we free your management team to focus on core business activities while we ensure the building operates at peak performance.
